Tools of the Trade



Introduction


In the final MultiVerse session we will be looking at different tools you may use in your role, including Big Data technologies and Data Analytics Platforms. We will consider the advantages of using such approaches and contrast them with the oding techniques you have learned over the previous sessions.

Following on from this, we will examine the Gateway process so you are aware of what to expect durig your End Point Assessment.



Learning Objectives


  • Understand fundamental concepts of Big Data
  • Justify the use (or lack of) Big Data technologies in your analysis
  • Critically evaluate the differences in using a Data Platform and Coding it Yourself
  • Understand the Gateway process
