Introduction


The first MultiVerse module is centered around the Data Analytics Lifecycle to help you 'think data' as you set out details, activities and roles throughout your work. You will be shown how to design effective Project Briefs to help you plan out your projects and communicate expectations to stakeholders.

In the second half of the module we will focus on data classification as we consider key characteristics such as structure, formats and types of data. This is followed by an in depth examination of GDPR and how we protect data privacy.



Learning Objectives


  • Describe the data analytics lifecycle and set out details, activities and roles at each stage in a real-world example
  • Design effective project briefs which communicate expectations to stakeholders
  • Identify business specific rules related to datasets and data characteristics that will influence project design and analysis
  • Identify Business Specific Rules related to datasets and data characteristics that will influence project design and analysis
  • Describe the key characteristics of the different Data Formats and how to work with them
