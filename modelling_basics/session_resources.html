Introduction


In this module we will show you how to build, refine and compare predictive models using sci-kit learn. We will focus on linear and logistic regression to build basic machine learning models as well as consider how to mitigate for bias and variance.



Learning Objectives


  • Build basic regression and classification models for analysis
  • Understand the outputs of models and explain what they mean to non technical stakeholders
  • Use cross validation and train test split to examine our models for bias and variance
